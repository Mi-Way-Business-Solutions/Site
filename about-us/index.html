About Us

Looking for someone who can listen and develop what you actually need? Here is a little bit about us and what we offer.

Who We Are

At Mi-Way Business Solutions, we build solutions around you. We're a Manchester-based startup with a simple goal: to help businesses run smarter with technology and design that fits your way of working.


Our team is made up of individuals with real experience across the industry — from working with Microsoft Business Central development partners to delivering full-scale digital design projects. We may be new as a company, but our expertise runs deep.


We specialise in platforms like Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, website development, and graphic design, but we're not limited to these. Whether it's a fully custom solution or one of our growing range of ready-made products, we’re here to build exactly what your business needs.

What We Do

We’ve worked inside the Microsoft partner network, consulted with all kinds of businesses, and seen firsthand what makes a solution actually useful. That knowledge fuels everything we build.

Business Central Development
Deep customisation and smart enhancements that make Business Central work harder for your team. We support:

  • Custom API integrations to connect with your wider tech stack
  • EDI workflows to automate B2B communication
  • Tailored functionality for finance, sales, purchasing, warehousing, and more

Web Design & Development
Modern, responsive websites designed to engage, convert, and grow with your business — from basic pages to full applications.


Graphic Design & Branding
Visual storytelling through clean, consistent, and impactful design. Logos, brand packs, marketing visuals — whatever you need.


We’re also developing our own software products, designed to solve real-world business pain points with simplicity and efficiency.
